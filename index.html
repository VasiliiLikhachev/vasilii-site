About

I’ve been a professional startup advisor and lead mentor since 2019, working at the intersection of product, growth, and investment. An active angel investor since 2021, I evaluate startups through the lens of unit economics, scalability, and execution viability.

I have served as a lead mentor, expert, and selection committee member for 30+ accelerators, including Google for Startups, Draper University, and Astana Hub. Beyond mentoring, I’ve built and managed corporate accelerators from scratch for market leaders like VkusVill and Sravni

Before moving into advisory, I was the CEO of a company with $50M+ annual revenue. This hands-on operational experience is my edge: I don't just advise I understand the grit it takes to build and scale a real business.
